What is CTE?

Career & Technical Education (CTE) is a subject offered here at Sunset, preparing the future generations for occupations involving strong command of technology. The field is growing rapidly, and we must fuel the flame by teaching all interested the essentials to fulfill their dreams. CTE includes most computer science, woodshop, and Family & Consumer Science courses.
