My Courses
Child Development

Child Development is a class in which students learn about the proper things needed for a child to properly develop. One of the main projects you will encounter in this class is taking home a robotic baby that you have to feed, burp, rock, and change its diaper. This program will simulate what it's like to have a baby. The baby will cry at any time, whether it's day or night. In this class, you will also do projects for a developing child, such as shape and color matching, emotion finding, etc.
