My Courses
Exploring Technology 1

This course will take you through the basics of working with wood and machinery. One of the major projects is working out the physics behind making a good rocket to be shot down the hallway out of papers and other materials. Another one involves the use of 3D pens to draw out a bridge after developing a sturdy structure on the computer.

Exploring Technology 2

This is an extension of Exploring Technology 1, where the skills you learned in the previous course are built upon. We will be using more tools than before, making loads of intriguing trinkets with them. Not only will you make a stunning cutting-board from scratch, but also learn how to use Adobe Illustrator utilizing the higher-end laptops available. Students will learn how to work as a team to make a physical object look stunning and act wonderfully.

Engineering Technology 1

As a third course in this series, it has a natural ramp up in difficulty. There is more of a focus on practical engineering in the real world, working with physics and whatnot. This class is perfect for any aspiring engineers looking to make either a career or hobby out of the skills developed here.

I love to design, build, and make.

I love to use all types of materials, tools, and machinery to end up with amazing products and inventions.

I love to help and teach students and individuals feel empowered by building and creating.
