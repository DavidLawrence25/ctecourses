Keyboarding

Keyboarding class is a whole class period when you go on typing websites and try to get your WPM up, when you're done practicing you take a test try to beat your last test score. when you first enter the class you grab a book, and go on word and type what section you're on. you also practice the right way to sit while typing, and to not look at the keyboard. (by Bently Alberts)
